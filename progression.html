Progression

The full chapter-by-chapter journey through Bloodbound. Reading this will spoil story beats and boss reveals.

Your Journey

I

The Cursed Village

The game opens on your burning village. A dying elder binds a blood rune to your palm — your curse, your power. Learn basic movement, blood bolts, and dodge rolling. Face the Village Guardian, a massive armoured giant protecting the village gate.

🩸 Lifesteal Unlocked +50 Blood Coins
II

The Hollow Dungeon

Beneath the village lies an ancient prison full of skeletal enemies and lava traps. The Hollow Beast lurks in the depths — a multi-phase monster that charges and enters a berserk state at low HP. First encounter with ranged mage enemies.

+100 Blood Coins Key of Crimson
III

The Crimson Castle

Infiltrate the kingdom that condemned your village. Navigate blood-soaked halls patrolled by elite knights and sorcerers. Face Lord Vael — a sorcerer-king who summons allies mid-fight. At the end, a choice: spare him or end him. This decision affects the final ending.

+150 Blood Coins Shop Tier II Unlocked
IV

The Blood Temple

A temple built to worship the Blood God himself. Four obelisks hold the key to the true ending — activate all four. The Blood Guardians are a crystalline elemental entity cycling through four phases: flame, stone, shadow, and blood. Sanguine Sacrifice unlocks after clearing this chapter.

☠ Sanguine Sacrifice Unlocked +200 Blood Coins
V

The Final Realm

The source of everything. The Blood God manifests in five brutal phases — orbs, fracture, absorb, desperation, and a final all-screen assault. The ending depends on your choices: spare Lord Vael AND activate all four obelisks for the true ending. Otherwise, you absorb the curse.

🏆 Game Complete +500 Blood Coins 3 Possible Endings

The Three Endings

✨

Break the Curse

True Ending

Spare Lord Vael AND activate all 4 obelisks in Chapter IV. The rune is broken. You are free.

⚖️

Partial Freedom

Partial Ending

Do one but not both. You weaken the Blood God but the curse lingers.

🩸

Absorb the Curse

Default Ending

Neither condition met. You defeat the Blood God and absorb his power — becoming the new curse.
