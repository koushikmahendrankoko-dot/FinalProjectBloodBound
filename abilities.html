Combat Mechanics

🛡️

Parry

Right-click just before an enemy hits to parry. A gold ring appears during the window. Success staggers the attacker and triggers a camera flash.

💨

Dodge Roll

Press Shift to roll in your movement direction. You have full invincibility frames during the roll. Leaves a blood-tinted afterimage trail.

💔

Blood Cost

Abilities spend your HP. You can cast an ability at 1 HP above cost — the rune won't let you kill yourself. Manage HP as both a health and resource bar.
