Combat Tips

🩸

Aim with your mouse

Blood bolts and directional abilities all fire toward your cursor. Keep your mouse pointed at enemies for maximum effectiveness.

🛡️

Parry timing

Right-click just before an enemy strikes — a gold ring appears when the window is active. A successful parry stuns the attacker for nearly a second.

💨

Roll through attacks

You have full invincibility frames during the entire dodge roll. Roll through projectiles, boss swings, and AoE attacks safely.

💔

Manage your HP resource

Abilities cost HP. Keep at least enough HP to cast your most important ability. The rune prevents self-kill but running dry means no abilities.

💀

Use Death Mark on bosses

Apply Death Mark first, then use your heaviest abilities. 3× damage multiplier turns a 300-damage Sanguine Sacrifice into 900 damage.

🌊

Save Blood Tide for crowds

Blood Tide pierces every enemy in a line. Save it for when multiple enemies are lined up, or fire it diagonally across the whole arena.

HUD Guide

❤️

HP Bar

Red bar = current HP. Yellow lag bar = recent damage (Elden Ring style drain). Low HP pulses a red vignette on screen.

🩸

Blood Coins

Earned by defeating enemies. Spend at the shop between runs. Persists across sessions when signed in.

⚡

Ability Slots

Shows cooldown as a fill overlay. Grey = on cooldown, full colour = ready. Dim = not enough HP to cast.

🗺️

Minimap

Bottom-right corner. Red dot = your position. White dots = chests. Darker tiles = walls.
